Designing a flashlight that ran off of pennies and water. This design was for the purpose of making a sustainable product that could reuse or repurpose material. One of the challenges that continued to arise when going through the initial stages of the designing process was creating something that was useful. The solution was designing a useful tool that is always included in emergency kits and transforming it into and easy recycled piece.
