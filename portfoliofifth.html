In this graphic piece, we were given the artistic freedom of designing a playful combination of letterforms that displayed movement and and flow to accentuate a specific type of typeface. One of the challenges I faced was positioning the letters in a manner that was interesting yet meaningful. One way that I found helped was using a sans-serif font and all caps letters. This allowed me to visualize the letters and better place them.
